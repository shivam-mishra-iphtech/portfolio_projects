The Challenge:

A Retail Giant Ready for Its Digital Chapter

Eureka had already mastered the in-store experience: diverse brands, entertaining environments, and unparalleled after-sales service. However, their digital presence needed to reflect this market leadership. Key challenges included:

  • A Fragmented Online Experience: The existing digital presence couldn't handle the complexity of their product catalog or their value-added services like installation and consumer financing.
  • Bridging the Online-Offline Gap: How could they bring their famous "customer-focused" values and comprehensive services into the digital realm?
  • Scalability for Growth: The platform needed to support their appetite for geographical and business diversification with a solid technological foundation.
  • Maintaining Brand Excellence: The digital experience had to feel every bit as premium, trustworthy, and comprehensive as a visit to an Eureka megastore.

Our Solution:

An Omnichannel Platform Built on Eureka's Values
We built a unified digital storefront that is a true extension of the Eureka brand, putting their mission to "enhance the life-style of consumers" at the very core of the user experience.

The Unified E-Commerce Platform

  • A high-performance, mobile-first website that showcases Eureka's vast international product diversity. We designed an intuitive user journey that makes discovering and comparing high-end electronics simple and engaging.

The Unified E-Commerce Platform

  • The platform seamlessly integrates Eureka's key differentiators:
  • Consumer Financing & Credit: Customers can apply for and manage financing directly during checkout.
  • After-Sales Service Scheduling: Easy booking for installation, maintenance, and extended warranty services.
  • Transparent Delivery Tracking: Real-time updates for free delivery and installation, bringing peace of mind.

The Advanced Admin Dashboard

  • A powerful backend system that gives Eureka's team total control. From centralized inventory management across all five stores to detailed customer insights and sales analytics, it empowers their people to make data-driven decisions.

The Results

Enhancing a Legacy with Digital Power

Quantifiable Outcomes:

  • Unified Digital Presence: Successfully launched a single platform representing all 5+ mega stores and their full service portfolio.
  • Streamlined Service Integration: Successfully digitised and integrated 5+ core after-sales services (financing, delivery, installation, etc.) into the purchase flow.
  • Increased Digital Engagement: Significant growth in online customer inquiries and service bookings, driving more value from each customer interaction.
  • Strong Foundation for Growth: Built a scalable platform ready to support Eureka's future geographical and business diversification.

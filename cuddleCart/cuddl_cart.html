The Challenge:

Innovation Held Back by Analog Processes

CuddleCart’s strollers had already changed the game. Their design allowed malls, shopping centers, and zoos to store 3x more strollers in the same space. But the rental system was stuck in the 1990s.

  • Parents wrestled with clunky vending machines that rejected bills or jammed mid-transaction. 
  • Strollers were often left scattered throughout venues, frustrating property managers. 
  • The cash-only payment system limited flexibility and excluded modern consumers. 
  • No tracking or accountability meant losses, maintenance issues, and low efficiency. 

As Kayvon Katoozian, Director of Innovation at CuddleCart, told us in our first meeting.

“Everyone has a smartphone. Why can’t renting a stroller be as simple as ordering an Uber?”

Our Solution:

The Cuddle Lock Ecosystem

We set out to reimagine the entire rental process—not just digitize it. The result was the Cuddle Lock Ecosystem, a seamless blend of mobile, IoT, and cloud technologies.

Cuddle Lock Mobile App

A native iOS & Android application designed for families on the go. With just a smartphone, parents can:

  • Scan a QR code on the stroller’s locking device
  • Unlock instantly via Bluetooth
  • Track rental duration
  • Return securely to a designated station

The entire process—from renting to returning—takes under 30 seconds

Smart Locking Device

  • Battery-powered (2-year life), weather-resistant lock designed for high-traffic venues
  • Communicates with the app via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)
  • Rental ends only when the stroller is physically locked back in place, solving the scatter problem once and for all

Cloud-Based Admin Panel

  • Real-time dashboard for monitoring rentals, availability, and revenue
  • Automated alerts for maintenance and unusual activity
  • Customisable pricing (time-based, event-based, demand-based)
  • Advanced analytics to track usage patterns, optimize operations, and boost ROI

Key Features & Strategic Impact

Frictionless Self-Service Rentals

Parents rent a stroller in seconds with their smartphone, anytime, without kiosks or staff intervention.

Guaranteed Returns

Rentals only end when the stroller is locked at a station, drastically reducing labor for collection and ensuring availability.

Diverse Revenue Models

Direct purchase or revenue-share options made the system attractive to venue partners.

Multi-Rental Capability

Families with more than one child can rent multiple strollers with one device, maximizing revenue per customer.
